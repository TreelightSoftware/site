About

Created by Chaplains for Chaplains, ChapTrack exists to make it easy to keep track of, report, and follow-up on all of your efforts. Let's face it: ministry is hard. ChapTrack can make the administration of your ministry easier. With modern state-of-the-art encryption, an easy-to-use interface, and new features regularly, ChapTrack is your personal ministry assistant!

Features

Activities

Activities are events or efforts you make in your day to day ministry. You can track time, participants, categories and tags.

Never forget what you did! Utilize your activity history to see where you are spending your time and effort to better optimize your ministry.

Reports

Use your activity data to generate a wide range of reports, filtered by category, date, and tags. If you need to submit reports of your efforts, ChapTrack can automatically generate and send reports in common formats for you!

Contacts

Keep track of your ministry contacts. Store their contact information and key notes that help you minister to them. Using the notes, you can easily lookup important information from previous counseling or ministry sessions!

Using our built-in, end-to-end encrypted technology, you can send completely confidential messages to any of your contacts. The messages are secured with a private key, which is unique for each message and account.

And More!

Reminders, data exports, messaging, contact grouping, and simple pricing makes ChapTrack the best technology assistant for busy spiritual leaders available on the market tody. New features are always being added!
