Our Products

Aside from helping our clients achieve success, we have created and operate several of our own products that demonstrate our capabilities and skills. Each one is successful and actively used.

KDojang

KDojang is a professional martial arts studio management system designed by martial arts instructors. KDojang was built from the ground up to provide martial arts instructors the ability to easily manage the administration of their school. We take care of contracts, invoices, curriculum, schedules, documents, and more! It is easy to use, entirely hosted on secure systems, and free to get started. You became an instructor to instruct. KDojang can handle the rest!

TLSchedule

TLSchedule was created during the early days of the COVID-19 pandemic. When schools, gyms, and martial arts studios began to reopen, there was a need to control how many people could join any specific class. Existing tools were lacking. They either didn't allow great control of upper limits, or didn't take family "pods" into account.

Enter TLSchedule. TLSchedule was designed from the ground up to make it easy to set up events and manage reservations. Families can sign up and be counted as a single "reservation". Maximum reservations per week can be set to allow fair reservations. Registration and set up are easy. With an intuitive interface, TLSchedule let's you get back in business safely.

With modern state-of-the-art encryption, an easy-to-use interface, and new features regularly, ChapTrack is your personal ministry assistant!
